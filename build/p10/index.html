Prototype10

High fidelity web prototypes built in 10 days

Service Introduction

The concept is quite simple.

I will design and build a high fidelity web prototype in 10 days. Pricing is variable. Most projects end up costing between $5k and $10k. A web prototype does not necessarily mean the subject matter of the prototype being web based. A web prototype simply means I will use web technologies (HTML/CSS/JS) to build the prototype. These prototypes can be used to demo web, mobile or native apps.

Product Value

Prototypes are the new MVP. They can be used to show a new feature or an entire new product.

1. Market Validation:

Does your company have a new idea for a product? Too busy with building your bread and butter to devote engineering cycles to a prototype? Let me work with your product team to build a prototype that you can take to conferences and meetings to test the idea.
